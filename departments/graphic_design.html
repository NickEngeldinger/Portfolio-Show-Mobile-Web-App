  • Cliburn, James

    "James Cliburn is a designer that works within the print and digital mediums, using them both to help create designs that fit with a client’s needs. This has also given him the skills needed to help a client develop their identity and help promote their brand through print collateral and the web. His main goal is to create designs that are high in quality, usable, accessible, and memorable. He is also an accomplished illustrator, drawing inspiration from the formal beauty of Modernism and the absurd thoughtfulness of Superflat – creating a unique style that has been described has having the same elegance found in 1960s cereal box designs. This is what drives James Cliburn as a person: to be quirky and fun but never losing the professionalism expected of a career creative. You can check out a selection of his work at jamescliburn.com – but make sure you stop by to see him while you’re at the Graduation Show!"

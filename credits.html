Hello, my name is Nick Engeldinger

I created this app using the Beta 2 version of jQuery Mobile, HTML5, CSS3 and some good old fashioned hard work. I coded it in Vim on Ubuntu, utilized Git for version control and the source code can be viewed on Github. My hope is that the app will be useful for the portfolio show attendees not only during the show but later as a tool to look up a graduate's information. There are many bright, hard working and ambitious students in the summer class of 2011 and I am proud to be graduating alongside them. I would like to take the oppurtunity to thank two of my instructors whom without their wisdom and dedication I would have never developed an app like this. Griff Walker and Peter Wooley taught me, guided me, supported me and inspired me. This app is not so much a demonstration of what I can do as it is a demonstration of the high level of instruction offered by these gentlemen.
